Workout for weight loss

Cardio Workout

30-45 minutes of running or cycling.

Strength Training

3 sets of squats, lunges, push-ups, and planks.

High-Intensity Interval Training (HIIT)

20-30 minutes of intense exercises like burpees and jumping jacks.

Flexibility and Stretching

Include yoga or Pilates in your routine.

Remember to consult a fitness professional before starting any exercise program.
